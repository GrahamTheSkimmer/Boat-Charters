About Captain Scott...

     Hello, my name is Captain Scott. I am a licenced Coast Guard 25 ton Master Captain and a 3rd generation native Floridian offering charters in Sarasota, Siesta Key , Venice and Anna Maria. I have been handed down the tradition and local knowledge of Florida waters by both my grandfathers and my father. I have been on the water since I could remember. I have a love for the outdoors and I know the local Sarasota waters like the back of my hand. Whether your an local expert or have never been to Florida I promise to give you all I've got to make sure you have a great day on the water.I have a passion for sharing our great outdoors to the next generation. I love sharing this passion with kids on the boat. The look on their face when they see a dolphin for the first time or watch a sunset from the water keeps me going. I pride myself in being patient, helpful, attentive and I promise to do everything I can to make your experience on the water a great one.
